# Gamma — AI Presentation Builder Setup Guide

Go from a topic or outline to a fully designed presentation deck in under 60 seconds.

## What It Is
Gamma generates complete slide decks, documents, and webpages from a text prompt or outline. It handles layout, design, and formatting automatically — no slide-by-slide building.

## Setup (Free)

1. Go to [gamma.app](https://gamma.app) and create a free account (Google sign-in works)
2. Click **+ New** → choose **Presentation**
3. Choose your starting method:
   - **Generate from prompt** — type a topic, Gamma builds it
   - **Paste an outline** — give it bullet points, it expands them into slides
   - **Import a document** — paste text or upload a PDF
4. Choose the number of slides (8–15 recommended)
5. Select a visual theme from the gallery
6. Click **Generate** — full deck appears in ~30 seconds
7. Edit any slide by clicking directly on it — text, images, layout all editable
8. Click **Present** to present live, or **Export** → PDF/PowerPoint

## Tips for Better Output
- More specific prompts = better decks. "Intro to volume profile trading for beginners, 10 slides" beats "trading presentation"
- Use **Paste Outline** if you already know your structure — gives you more control
- Images are AI-generated by default — you can swap any image by clicking it
- The **Card** format (scrollable webpage) often looks better than traditional slides for sharing links
