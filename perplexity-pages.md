# Perplexity Pages — Setup Guide

Turn any topic into a polished, shareable research page in under 2 minutes.

## What It Is
Perplexity Pages lets you create structured, source-cited pages on any topic. Think of it as AI-generated Wikipedia entries you control and share.

## Setup (Free)

1. Go to [perplexity.ai](https://perplexity.ai) and create a free account
2. Click **Pages** in the left sidebar (or go to perplexity.ai/pages)
3. Click **+ Create a Page**
4. Enter your topic — be specific (e.g. "How ES futures volume profile works" not just "trading")
5. Choose your audience: Beginner / Intermediate / Expert
6. Click **Generate** — Perplexity builds the full page with sources in ~30 seconds
7. Edit any section by clicking directly on it
8. Click **Publish** → copy the shareable link

## Tips
- Add your own sections after generation using the **+** button
- Toggle source citations on/off per section
- Pages are public by default — great for link-in-bio content
- Use "Expert" audience level for technical topics to avoid dumbed-down output

## Pro Upgrade ($20/mo)
Unlocks unlimited pages, custom domains, and priority generation. Free tier allows ~3 pages/day.
