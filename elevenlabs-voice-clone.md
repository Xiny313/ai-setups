# ElevenLabs Voice Clone — Setup Guide

Clone your own voice in under 5 minutes. Use it for voiceovers without recording every time.

## What It Is
ElevenLabs lets you upload 1–3 minutes of audio and create a clone of any voice. Your clone can then read any text you type, in your voice.

## Setup

1. Go to [elevenlabs.io](https://elevenlabs.io) and create a free account
2. In the left sidebar, click **Voices** → **Add a New Voice**
3. Select **Instant Voice Clone** (free tier)
4. Upload audio samples:
   - Minimum: 1 minute of clean audio
   - Best: 2–3 minutes, no background noise, natural speech
   - Works with: MP3, WAV, M4A
   - Tip: record yourself reading anything out loud — articles, scripts, anything
5. Name your voice clone and click **Add Voice**
6. Go to **Speech Synthesis** in the sidebar
7. Select your cloned voice from the dropdown
8. Type or paste any text → click **Generate**
9. Download the MP3

## Recording Tips for Best Clone Quality
- Use your phone's voice memo app in a quiet room
- Speak naturally — vary your pace, don't be robotic
- Avoid filler words (uh, um) — the clone inherits them
- 3 minutes beats 1 minute significantly for accuracy
